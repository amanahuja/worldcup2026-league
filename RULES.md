## Predictions and Scores (details) 

Predict the results of matches during the World Cup taking place June-July 2026. 
* For every match in the tournament — all 104 of them — you predict the winner, or a draw (group stage only). 
* You don't predict scorelines, just outcomes.
* Every bracket is pre-filled with a default prediction based on FIFA seedings — the higher-ranked team wins, or a draw where teams are closely ranked. If you want, you don't have to change anything at all. 
* You can change any or all predictions up until each prediction window closes. Changes save automatically — there's no submit button.
* Default picks and picks you make yourself score identically. There is no bonus for changing a default.
* Later games are worth more points: 1 point for group stage result, 10 points for the final game, etc. 
* The tie breaker is the best heckler on the groupme chat. We'll use the groupme poll / vote for "best heckler" after the tournament is over. 
* There is a prediction for the score of the final (last game). This will be used as a secondary and more objective tie breaker, if needed. 

For knockout matches that go to extra time or penalties: only the overall match winner matters. It doesn't matter how they won.


### Prediction deadlines

There are three windows, each with its own deadline:

| Window | Covers | Locks |
|---|---|---|
| Group Stage | All 72 group matches | June 11 — first kickoff |
| Knockout | R32 through the Final (31 matches) | June 29 — first R32 kickoff |
| Third Place | 1 match | ~July 16 — when both semi-finals are confirmed |

Once a window locks, your picks are frozen. You can still view them, but you can't change them.

### Scoring

Points are awarded for correctly predicting the outcome of each match:

| Match | Points for correct prediction |
|---|---|
| Group stage match (win or draw) | 1 pt |
| Round of 32 | 2 pts |
| Round of 16 | 3 pts |
| Quarter-final | 4 pts |
| Semi-final | 5 pts |
| Third-place playoff | 3 pts |
| Final | 10 pts |

**Maximum possible score: 91 points**
(72 × 1 + 16 × 2 + 8 × 3 + 4 × 4 + 2 × 5 + 1 × 3 + 1 × 10)
